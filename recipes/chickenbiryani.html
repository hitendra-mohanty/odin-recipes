Chicken biryani

Chicken biryani is a delicious Pakistani/Indian rice dish that's typically reserved for special occasions such as weddings, parties, or holidays such as Ramadan.

It has a lengthy preparation, but the work is definitely worth it.

For biryani, basmati rice is the ideal variety to use.

INGREDENTS

  • Rice
  • onion
  • garlic
  • chicken
  • etc

HOW TO MAKE CHCIKEN BIRYANI

  1. step 1-Boil rice with dry spcies
  2. step 2-fry onions and make chicken curry
  3. step 3-add rice in layers in the chicken curry
