Lasagna

This is my mom's special homemade lasagna recipe with a meaty, made-from-scratch tomato sauce and a deliciously cheesy. A more traditional homemade LASAGNA filling would be made with ricotta but my mom's recipe calls for a blend of small-curd cottage cheese and Parmesan. I have found none better anywhere. Serve with a leafy green salad and crusty garlic bread.

INGREDENTS

  • meat
  • onion
  • canned tomatoes
  • sugar
  • fresh herbs
  • spices and seasonings
  • cheese
  • eggs
  • noodles

HOW TO MAKE HOMEMADE LASAGNA

  1. cook the meat
  2. cook the noodles
  3. make the cheese layer
  4. assemble the lasagna
  5. bake the lasagna
